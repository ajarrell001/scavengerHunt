Scavenger Hunt

Instructions

You have begun the scavenger hunt set within the city of Philadelphia. There are three landmark locations you must visit to win the game. You will start with one clue for the first landmark. Once you find a landmark, you will get a message and a clue for the next landmark. Once you have successfully found the third landmark, you win the game. When you are ready to go to the location, turn on tracking.
